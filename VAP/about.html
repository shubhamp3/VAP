Autosar

Why AUTOSAR?

The AUTOSAR is an open and standardized layered automotive software architecture jointly developed by automobile manufacturers, suppliers and tool developers. The scope of AUTOSAR includes all the vehicle domains. If the ECU software is not standardized it becomes almost impossible to integrate the ECU to complex vehicle network infrastructure. Then it becomes costlier and complex. Autosar gives increased flexibility and scalability to transfer and integrate functions, cost optimization of scalable systems, flexibility for product modification and updates, improved reliability and quality of E/E systems.
